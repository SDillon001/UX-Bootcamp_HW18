My journey as an artist and designer has spanned most of my life. I began like many - doodling in my notebook turned into taking formal art lessons, which inspired me to study Art at University. I earned my B.A. in Studio Art, concentrating on the traditional disciplines of painting, drawing, printmaking and photography and fell in love with Art.

Starting out in the Graphic Design industry during the final year of college, my first Design mentor taught me the fundamentals of digital design and I learned how to learn by reading all of the software manuals for the top design programs in use at the time.

After moving from New England to Southern California to pursue competitive surfing, my career took a detour. I became a Senior Analyst for a large property management firm where I learned how to work as part of a team to find solutions to large scale problems.

Guided by my love for Art and Design, I went back to school to complete a Master’s degree program in Web Design & New Media, honing my UX/UI, Web Design, and MoGraph skills. I’ve been a professional designer now for over 5 years.

In that time I’ve conducted hours of user research, countless usability tests, designed and deployed hundreds of websites and worked on the design teams for some amazing global companies, SaaS tech startups, and design agencies. I've also been very fortunate to work one-on-one with freelance design clients.

I'm grateful for a successful career pursuing my passion for solving people's problems with digital solutions - one that drives me to push my creativity every day.
